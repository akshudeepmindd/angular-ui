About Us
Raviolis

In the popular village of ravioli, on the outskirts of Leicester city centre, Aspects is a fantastic fusion restaurant combining the very best of Authentic Fine Indian Cuisine along with British cuisine(Lunch time only). A La carte fine dining is served in a elegant newly refurbished restaurant giving you a experience of Dining in India is served in the newly launched dining area giving you the experience and feel of being on Indian streets.

The restaurant is bright and attractive, with a style where traditional and contemporary features are blended. The stylish exposed red brickwork wall gives the restaurant a kick of colour and design, contrasted with the elegant and neutral exposed wooden ceiling beams. The restaurant is spacious but cosy, giving it a fantastic atmosphere of an evening and allowing for a sociable, buzzing environment ideal for groups, celebrations and catching up with friends. Or perhaps choose a quieter part of the restaurant to sit and the excellent food and wonderful customer service top off a fantastic choice of restaurant for a romantic or intimate meal.

TAt lunch time, the Express Lunch menu is a firm favourite for flavour and affordability. Enjoy a flavoursome Indian Platter serving two people with tandoori chicken, chicken tikka, lamb tikka and seekh kebab, all served alongside rice or naan, curry dips and fresh salad. There’s a fantastic variety of baguettes, wraps and jacket potatoes available too, such as cheese and beans, Cajun chicken or prawns in a marie rose sauce. Of an evening, choose from the fantastic Indian A La Carte menu. To start, choose something bursting with flavour such as garlic mushrooms, cooked with a blend of spices and finely chopped chillies, followed by one of the chef’s specialities such as a Karai Chicken curry, cooked with tomatoes, coriander and crunchy peppers. All the favourites are on the menu too, including Mutter Paneer, a dish of cubed cottage cheese cooked in green peas.
